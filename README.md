Overview
StratAI is an AI strategy simulation platform designed to model enterprise AI adoption decisions using a weighted multi-criteria prioritization framework.
The tool enables structured evaluation of AI initiatives across key dimensions such as business impact, data readiness, implementation complexity, risk exposure, and strategic alignment.
It simulates how consulting teams and executive leadership prioritize AI transformation roadmaps.
Problem Statement
Enterprises often struggle to objectively prioritize AI initiatives due to competing strategic priorities, risk tolerance differences, and resource constraints.
StratAI addresses this challenge by providing:
Quantitative scoring engine
Adjustable strategic weighting
Phase-based roadmap generation
Executive-level visualization
Key Features
Weighted multi-factor AI prioritization model
Adjustable strategic weight sliders (scenario modeling)
Risk-adjusted scoring logic
Automatic AI adoption roadmap generation
Executive summary auto-generation
Interactive bar and pie chart dashboards
Persistent local storage
